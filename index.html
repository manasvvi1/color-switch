How to Play

1.The ball will bounce upwards on mouse click.

2.Hurdles will appear with color changing time to time.

3.The ball can pass the hurdle only when the color of the ball and the hurdle is same.

4.The player can collect circular coins present inside the hurdle.

5.The squares are colorchangers, which will allow the player to change the color of the ball.
